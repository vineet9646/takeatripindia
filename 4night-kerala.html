Kerala Packages

4 Night Kerala

Suggested: Itinerary! (Munnar 2nt + Thekkady 1nt + Alleppey 1nt)

Cochin Airport – Munnar Sightseeing

Day 01:  In assistance on arrival at Cochin Airport take proceed to the airport and drive to Munnar form Cochin, which is about 130 km away. Check-in to your hotel and relax. Munnar is a hill station in Kerala, India, known for its tea plantations and exotic flora and fauna.

Later visit the Tata Tea Museum (KDHP Museum) to learn about the history and culture of tea production in Munnar. In the evening, visit the Blossom Park, a beautiful garden with a variety of flowers, plants and trees. You can also enjoy some activities like boating, cycling, roller skating and zip lining here. Overnight stay at Munnar.

Munnar - Sightseeing

Day 02:  After breakfast, visit the Eravikulam National Park to see the Nilgiri Tahr and the Neelakurinji flowers. You can also enjoy a panoramic view of the Anamudi peak from here.

In the afternoon, visit the Mattupetty Dam and enjoy boating on the lake. You can also visit the Echo Point and Photo Point nearby for some scenic views and fun activities. Overnight stay at Munnar

Munnar – Thekkady Sightseeing

Day 03:  Post breakfast and drive to Thekkady form Munnar, which is about 85 to 90 km by road. Check-in to your hotel and relax. Thekkady is a wildlife destination in Kerala, India, known for its Periyar National Park and Tiger Reserve.

Later visit the Periyar National Park and Tiger Reserve (tickets cost extra) and enjoy boating on the Periyar Lake. Later visit Gavi, a pristine eco-tourism spot that is located inside the Periyar Tiger Reserve. You can enjoy the natural beauty and wildlife of Gavi by camping, trekking, bird watching, boating and jeep safari.

In the afternoon, visit the Abraham’s Spice Garden and learn about the various spices and herbs grown there. You can also buy some organic products from the shop. Later return to hotel. Overnight stay at Thekkady

Thekkady – Alleppey - Sightseeing

Day 04:  After breakfast, Drive from Thekkady to Alleppey, this is about 140 km away. Check-in to your houseboat or Hotel and relax. Alleppey, or Alappuzha, is a coastal town in Kerala, India, known for its backwaters, houseboats, beaches and temples.

Later visit Alappuzha Beach, a popular beach that offers a blend of history, culture and nature. You can see the 150-year-old pier, the lighthouse, the Vijaya Beach Park and the Sea View Park here. You can also enjoy activities like swimming, surfing, and parasailing and beach volleyball. Later return to hotel. Overnight stay at Alleppey houseboat

Alleppey – Cochin Airport Drop

Day 05:  After breakfast proceed to Cochin airport and depart. Travel back to your home with wonderful memories of amazing experience in Kerala.

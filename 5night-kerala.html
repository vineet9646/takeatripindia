Kerala Packages

5 Night Kerala

Suggested: Itinerary! ( Cochin 1nt + Munnar 2nt + Thekkady 1nt + Alleppey 1nt)

Cochin Airport Pick up – Sightseeing

Day 01:  In assistance on arrival at Cochin Airport at morning and drop to Hotel. Cochin, also known as Kochi, is a vibrant city in Kerala that offers a blend of history, culture, nature and cuisine.

On the first day, you can start your trip by visiting the Ernakulum Shiva Temple, one of the ancient temples in central Kerala. You can witness the rituals and ceremonies performed by the priests and devotees

Next, you can head to the Jewish Synagogue and the Jew Town, which are among the oldest Jewish settlements in India. You can admire the architecture of the synagogue and shop for antiques and spices in the town. You can also have some coffee and snacks at a cozy cafe

After that, you can visit the Dutch Palace, also known as the Mattancherry Palace, which displays the portraits of the Rajas of Kochi and some exquisite murals

Then, you can proceed to the Fort Kochi Beach where you can see the iconic Chinese fishing nets and the ruins of Fort Emmanuel. You can also enjoy a delicious lunch at one of the nearby restaurants

In the evening, you can watch an enthralling Kathakali performance at the Kerala Kathakali Centre (tickets not included), which showcases the traditional dance-drama of Kerala. Overnight stay at Cochin

Cochin – Munnar - Sightseeing

Day 02:  Post breakfast, drive to Munnar form Cochin, which is about 130 km away. Check-in to your hotel and relax. Munnar is a hill station in Kerala, India, known for its tea plantations and exotic flora and fauna.

Later visit the Tata Tea Museum (KDHP Museum) to learn about the history and culture of tea production in Munnar.

In the evening, visit the Blossom Park, a beautiful garden with a variety of flowers, plants and trees. You can also enjoy some activities like boating, cycling, roller skating and zip lining here. Overnight stay at Munnar.

Munnar - Sightseeing

Day 03:  After breakfast, visit the Eravikulam National Park to see the Nilgiri Tahr and the Neelakurinji flowers. You can also enjoy a panoramic view of the Anamudi peak from here.

In the afternoon, visit the Mattupetty Dam and enjoy boating on the lake. You can also visit the Echo Point and Photo Point nearby for some scenic views and fun activities. Overnight stay at Munnar.

Munnar –Thekkady Sightseeing

Day 04:  Post breakfast and drive to Thekkady form Munnar, which is about 85 to 90 km by road. Check-in to your hotel and relax. Thekkady is a wildlife destination in Kerala, India, known for its Periyar National Park and Tiger Reserve.

Later visit the Periyar National Park and Tiger Reserve (tickets cost extra) and enjoy boating on the Periyar Lake.

Later visit Gavi, a pristine eco-tourism spot that is located inside the Periyar Tiger Reserve. You can enjoy the natural beauty and wildlife of Gavi by camping, trekking, bird watching, boating and jeep safari (all activities own cost).

In the afternoon, visit the Abraham’s Spice Garden and learn about the various spices and herbs grown there. You can also buy some organic products from the shop. Later return to hotel. Overnight stay at Thekkady.

Thekkady – Alleppey - Sightseeing

Day 05:  After breakfast, Drive from Thekkady to Alleppey, this is about 140 km away. Check-in to your houseboat or Hotel and relax. Alleppey, or Alappuzha, is a coastal town in Kerala, India, known for its backwaters, houseboats, beaches and temples.

Later visit Alappuzha Beach, a popular beach that offers a blend of history, culture and nature. You can see the 150-year-old pier, the lighthouse, the Vijaya Beach Park and the Sea View Park here. You can also enjoy activities like swimming, surfing, and parasailing and beach volleyball. Later return to hotel. Overnight stay at Alleppey.

Alleppey - Cochin Airport Drop (90 Km away)

Day 06: After a hearty breakfast at the hotel, you will be transfer to the airport in time for your flight back home.
